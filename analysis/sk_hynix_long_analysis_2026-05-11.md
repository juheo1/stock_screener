# SK hynix (KRX:000660) Long Position Fundamental Analysis

Prepared: May 11, 2026 Korea trading date. If viewing from Indiana/US time, this corresponds to the evening of May 10, 2026.

This is a fundamental analysis memo, not personalized financial advice.

## Bottom Line

I would not chase a full new long position today at roughly KRW 1,875,000. The company fundamentals are excellent, but the stock already reflects a large amount of the good news.

My estimated 12-month fundamental fair-value target is KRW 1,900,000 per share, with a stretch/bull case around KRW 2,300,000 if the AI memory shortage and HBM pricing remain stronger for longer.

A better risk-adjusted entry zone is KRW 1,550,000 to KRW 1,650,000. At KRW 1,875,000, upside to my base target is only about 1%, while downside in a memory-cycle reset could be much larger. If you still want exposure today, a small starter position is more defensible than a full-size entry.

## Valuation

My base-case target uses a normalized forward EPS approach:

- Q1 2026 EPS power is very high, but I haircut the run-rate because memory profitability can reverse quickly.
- Normalized FY2026/FY2027 EPS assumption: KRW 150,000 per share.
- Fair multiple: 12.5x, reflecting better structural HBM demand but still acknowledging cyclicality.
- Add net cash per share: about KRW 50,000.

Calculation:

```text
KRW 150,000 normalized EPS x 12.5 = KRW 1,875,000
+ KRW 50,000 net cash per share
= KRW 1,925,000
Rounded target: KRW 1,900,000
```

Scenario range:

- Bear case: KRW 1,050,000 to KRW 1,250,000 if DRAM/NAND pricing normalizes faster than expected or capex overshoots demand.
- Base case: KRW 1,850,000 to KRW 1,950,000.
- Bull case: KRW 2,250,000 to KRW 2,350,000 if HBM shortages persist, customer prepayments improve visibility, and margins stay elevated.

## Investment View

Fundamentally, SK hynix is a high-quality AI memory winner right now. The balance sheet has flipped to net cash, demand visibility is strong, and the company is investing aggressively in EUV, M15X, and Yongin capacity to defend its HBM/advanced DRAM position.

The issue is price. At around KRW 1,875,000, the stock is already near its 52-week high and above the average analyst target I found. That makes the entry setup unattractive unless you have a very strong bull-case view that current margins are not peak-ish.

Decision:

- New full long today: No, not attractive on risk/reward.
- Small starter position: Reasonable only for high-conviction, high-volatility tolerance investors.
- Better entry: KRW 1,550,000 to KRW 1,650,000.
- Base target: KRW 1,900,000.
- Stretch target: KRW 2,300,000.
- Fundamental invalidation watch: HBM share loss, weaker AI server demand, DRAM/NAND spot price reversal, aggressive industry capex, or customer pushback against memory pricing.
